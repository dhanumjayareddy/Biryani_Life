The Craft

Not cooked.
Composed.

Each layer of STAR BIRYANI biryani is a decision made hours before it reaches you — saffron dissolved in warm milk, whole spices hand-bloomed in ghee, meat sealed under the dum until time does its work.

01

Saffron Infusion

Premium Kashmiri saffron steeped overnight in warm cream, threaded through every grain.

02

Sealed Under Dum

Atta-dough sealed lids trap the aroma for hours — you smell it before you open the box.

03

Fire Finishing

A final coal or live flame pass to deepen the smokiness and seal the crust of rice.
