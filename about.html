A Legacy of Flavor

From the royal kitchens of Hyderabad to the heart of America, our journey is one of tradition, passion, and the art of perfect biryani.

Our Heritage

Born in Tradition, Perfected in America

STAR BIRYANIbegan not as a restaurant, but as a promise—a promise to bring the authentic taste of Hyderabadi dum biryani to those who crave true culinary artistry. Our founder, a third-generation chef from Hyderabad, grew up watching his grandmother and mother perfect the ancient art of dum cooking.

The name "STAR BIRYANI" comes from "STAR BIRYANI"—the master chef who has perfected the art of dum cooking, where meat and rice are sealed together and slow-cooked over low heat, allowing the aromas and flavors to meld into something magical.

In 2024, we opened our first restaurant in the United States, bringing with us centuries-old recipes, traditional copper handis, and an unwavering commitment to authenticity. Every biryani we serve is a tribute to our heritage and a celebration of the royal cuisine that once graced the tables of Nizams.
